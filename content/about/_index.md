## What I Do

I bridge the gap between raw data and business decisions. My work spans three layers:

- **Data Engineering** — I build pipelines that move and transform data reliably. Using Airflow for orchestration, DBT for transformations, PostgreSQL for storage, and FastAPI for serving, I create systems that run without manual intervention.
- **Analytics & Visualization** — I design Power BI dashboards and Excel reports with DAX-based KPIs, variance analysis, and trend tracking that give stakeholders a clear picture of business performance.
- **Domain Expertise** — With a BS in Accounting and 3+ years of experience managing GL, AP/AR, reconciliations, and budgets in Saudi Arabia and the Philippines, I understand the business questions behind the data.

## Featured Project: Automated Data Pipeline for Financial Analytics

My most technically comprehensive project. An end-to-end system that:

1. Ingests financial source data into PostgreSQL
2. Transforms it through DBT models with documented lineage
3. Orchestrates daily runs via Apache Airflow
4. Validates and serves processed data through FastAPI
5. Feeds Power BI where I built an interactive financial dashboard published to Power BI Service

This is the kind of system I build: reliable, documented, and tied to real business outcomes.
