---
title: Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies
type: recipe
date: 2026-05-12
subtitle: Chewy, golden, and loaded with chocolate chips
tags: ["baking", "dessert", "cookies"]
recipe:
  prepTime: "PT15M"
  cookTime: "PT12M"
  totalTime: "PT27M"
  yield: "24 cookies"
  category: "Dessert"
  cuisine: "American"
  calories: "180 kcal"
  ingredients:
    - "2¼ cups all-purpose flour"
    - "1 tsp baking soda"
    - "1 tsp salt"
    - "1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, softened"
    - "¾ cup granulated sugar"
    - "¾ cup packed brown sugar"
    - "2 large eggs"
    - "2 tsp vanilla extract"
    - "2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips"
  instructions:
    - name: "Preheat oven"
      text: "Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C)."
    - name: "Mix dry ingredients"
      text: "In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt."
    - name: "Cream butter and sugars"
      text: "In a large bowl, beat butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until creamy."
    - name: "Add eggs and vanilla"
      text: "Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each. Stir in vanilla."
    - name: "Combine"
      text: "Gradually blend in the flour mixture. Stir in chocolate chips."
    - name: "Bake"
      text: "Drop rounded tablespoons onto ungreased baking sheets. Bake 9–12 minutes until golden brown."
    - name: "Cool"
      text: "Cool on baking sheets for 2 minutes, then transfer to wire racks."
---

A timeless favorite — these chocolate chip cookies come out perfectly chewy every time. The secret is in the ratio of brown to white sugar and letting the dough rest if you have the patience.
